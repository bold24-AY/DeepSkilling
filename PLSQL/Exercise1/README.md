# Exercise 1: Control Structures

## Scenarios
- **Scenario 1**: Loops through customers to apply a 1% interest rate discount to loans for customers over 60 years old.
- **Scenario 2**: Iterates through customers and updates `IsVIP` flag to TRUE for customers with a balance exceeding $10,000.
- **Scenario 3**: Identifies loans due in the next 30 days and outputs reminder messages for the customers.
